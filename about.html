🚀 About PrepSync AI

Building The Future Of Placement Preparation

PrepSync AI is an intelligent placement preparation platform designed to help students track progress, improve skills, prepare for interviews, and achieve career goals through smart analytics and AI-powered guidance.

Our Purpose

Empowering students with structured preparation, smart analytics, and future AI-powered career guidance.

Our Mission

To simplify placement preparation by bringing DSA tracking, interview preparation, resume analysis, and career planning into one platform.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted AI-powered platform helping students achieve career success and placement excellence.

Product Roadmap

A step-by-step vision showing how PrepSync AI will evolve from a placement platform into a complete career preparation ecosystem.

Phase 1

Landing Page

Build a modern and responsive platform introduction with a strong user experience and professional design.

Phase 2

Placement Dashboard

Track DSA progress, coding sheets, study goals, preparation analytics, and consistency.

Phase 3

AI Interview Simulator

Practice HR and technical interviews with personalized AI-generated feedback.

Phase 4

Career Ecosystem

Combine preparation tracking, resume analysis, roadmaps, and AI guidance into one platform.

The Problem We Are Solving

Placement preparation is often scattered across multiple platforms, making it difficult for students to stay consistent and measure progress.

Scattered Resources

Students switch between coding platforms, notes, spreadsheets, and learning websites.

No Progress Tracking

Most learners don't have a clear picture of their preparation journey and consistency.

Unstructured Preparation

Without a roadmap, students often focus on the wrong topics and waste valuable time.
